Privacy Policy – Hardskin Challenges

Effective date: August 27, 2025

At Hardskin Challenges, we value your privacy. This policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What We Collect

When you authorize the app via Strava, we access:

  • Your public Strava profile (name, avatar)
  • Public activity and segment effort data

We do not access private activities, health data, or personal location info beyond public segment data.

2. How We Use Data

We use it to:

  • Display leaderboards
  • Track challenge completions
  • Award badges

We never sell or share your data.

3. Deletion & Rights

To delete your data, contact us at info@hardskin.it. You can revoke access from your Strava settings anytime.
